Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

Information We Collect

BAYNE collects minimal data necessary to provide the AR MMORPG experience, including device identifiers, gameplay data, and spatial mapping data processed locally on your device.

How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain the game experience
  • To enable multiplayer features and persistent world state
  • To improve game performance and fix bugs
  • To communicate updates and events

Spatial Data

Room mapping and spatial data captured by Apple Vision Pro is processed entirely on-device. We do not upload, store, or share your spatial environment data on our servers.

Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your personal information. All data transmission is encrypted using TLS.

Third-Party Services

We may use third-party analytics and crash reporting tools. These services collect anonymized usage data to help us improve the game.

Children's Privacy

BAYNE is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 years of age.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at contact@playbayne.com.
